Terms And Conditions


    Koua is a mobile application that facilitates the link between craftsmen and possible costumers, which involves the manipulation of data of both types of users. The main purpose of this policy is to specify the privacy and security of the users and the manipulation of the information entered to the app.



    1. Kind of information recollected


    The personal information that is expected to be received from the users is divided into two different categories depending on the profile of the user. The two types of profiles that Koua offers according to the in the interests and characteristics of the user are:



    Craftsmen


    The information requested in this profile is wider that the Shopper profile. The main aim of this information is being accessible to the shoppers in order to facilitate the contact and communication between the people who is interested in the products and the craftsman who elaborates them.



    Requested data:


    -Name
    -Email
    -Password
    -Phone
    -Cellphone
    -Store Address



    Shopper


    The information requested in order to create an account in the section of shoppers is used to identify the user, personalize the profile, and login to the app.



    Requested data:


    -User Name
    -Email
    -Password



    2. Use of the information


    The personal content entered in the app will be used in different forms depending on the type of user.
    The information of the craftsmen will be displayed publically in the app and will be available to all kind of users in order to make the link and achieve the purpose of facilitate the relation offer and demand of handcrafts.
    The profile of the craftsmen will be public and can be seen by other users that may be interested in the articles displayed.
    The information obtained from the shoppers can be seen only by the user who uploads it, and the profile will be private too.



    3. Privacy


    -All the information requested will be used only for identifying the users and promote the link between the two types of profiles. -This information will not be shared with other people of companies.





    *The purpose of the password is to guarantee the privacy of the accounts of the users.
